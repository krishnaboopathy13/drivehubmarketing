FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Does it really work offline?▼
Yes. All data stored locally in IndexedDB. Syncs automatically when reconnected.
How do candidates register?▼
Share your drive link via WhatsApp, Teams, QR code. Candidates sign in with Google/LinkedIn/GitHub, fill form, get a token.
What assessment platforms integrate?▼
HackerRank, Codility, HackerEarth, Mettl, TestGorilla, Coderbyte, iMocha + custom REST APIs.
Is there a free plan?▼
7-day free trial, full access, no credit card. Then Pro ($499/mo) or Enterprise ($1,999/mo).
Can I import from spreadsheets?▼
Pro plan includes bulk CSV/Excel upload with smart column mapping and one-click import.
Do I need to install anything?▼
No. Runs in any browser. Optionally add to home screen.
